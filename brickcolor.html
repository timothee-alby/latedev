BRICKCOLOR

BrickColor is a simple and addictive iPhone game.

You just need to think a little bit, and to wisely choose the colors you pick. The main goal is to color all the squares in a minimum number of hits.

In Classic mode, take your time. But in Time Attack mode you have to think quickly.

Enjoy 5 levels, two game mode and two graphics styles !

Compete with your friends and the entire world on GameCenter !

Available for free on the AppStore, give it a try!

Have colorful fun :)
